Meningitis Location Prediction in Nigeria

To help the government curb the outbreak of Meningitis in Nigeria, we developed this AI model that predicts the location of an infected person in any Nigerian State. Data needed includes gender, month of meningitis confirmation, age, adult group, serotype and patient's life status. The AI runs the details through 74 pretrained models and generates results in the form of a Map, Bar Plot and Table. Click on the form button below to begin.
